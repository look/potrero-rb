Welcome to Potrero.rb

Potrero.rb is a Ruby Brigade on the model of Seattle.rb. We organize a weekly hack night where you can hack on your own project or help others with theirs. Unlike other meetup groups in town, we do not organize talks. Check out SFRails or SFRuby for that.

To attend Potrero.rb, bring a project to work on (it's a great chance to do some open source hacking). With everyone working on a project, every Potrero.rb meeting is a success no matter how many people show up. All are welcome, even if you have the misfortune of not living or working in Potrero Hill.

Weekly Hack Night

We meet at Steep Brew (the Whole Foods cafe) at 17th and Rhode Island from 7-9pm every Monday. Steep Brew has coffee, beer, food, and wifi (with purchase) so bring your thirst and laptop. There is no need to RSVP, just show up.

Bring a project to work on. Work on whatever you like, but here are some ideas:

  • An open source library (check out Seattle.rb's impressive list).
  • An article you've been meaning to work on.
  • Homework for your MOOC.
  • The Potrero.rb website.

Inspiration

This group is inspired by Miles Forrest's RailsConf 2013 lightning talk on cloning the Seattle Ruby brigade. As related by Miles, the three secrets to Seattle.rb's success are:

  1. Forget presentations. Do hack nights.
  2. Bring a project.
  3. Meet every week.

Potrero.rb exists to give Rubyists in the Potrero area a place to meet each other and get stuff done.
